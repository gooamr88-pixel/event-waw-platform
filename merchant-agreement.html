1 Definitions

In this Merchant Agreement ("Agreement"), the following terms apply:

  • "Platform" refers to Event Waw, including all websites, applications, APIs, and services.
  • "Merchant" refers to any event organizer, vendor, exhibitor, or sponsor who creates, manages, or sells goods/services through the Platform.
  • "Attendee" refers to any individual who purchases tickets or registers for events via the Platform.
  • "Service Fee" refers to the percentage or flat fee charged by Event Waw per transaction.
  • "Payout" refers to the transfer of earned revenue from Event Waw to the Merchant.

2 Merchant Eligibility

To use the Platform as a Merchant, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age or the legal age of majority in your jurisdiction.
  • Provide accurate and complete business or personal information during registration.
  • Have the legal authority to organize, host, or sell at the events you list.
  • Comply with all applicable local, national, and international laws and regulations.
  • Maintain a valid payment account capable of receiving payouts (bank account or payment provider).

Event Waw reserves the right to reject or revoke Merchant status at its sole discretion.

3 Merchant Obligations

As a Merchant on Event Waw, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate, complete, and up-to-date event information (name, date, time, venue, pricing, capacity).
  • Honor all tickets, reservations, and commitments made through the Platform.
  • Respond to attendee inquiries within 48 hours.
  • Ensure event venues comply with safety codes, accessibility requirements, and local regulations.
  • Not engage in misleading advertising, fake reviews, or deceptive practices.
  • Maintain adequate insurance for events where applicable.
  • Use the QR code verification system provided by the Platform for check-in.

4 Fees & Payment Terms

Event Waw charges the following fees for use of the Platform:

  • Platform Service Fee: A percentage of each ticket sale, clearly displayed to the Merchant before event publication.
  • Payment Processing Fee: Stripe's standard processing fee applies to all transactions.
  • Premium Features: Optional paid features (e.g., promoted listings, advanced analytics) are billed separately.

All fees are non-refundable unless otherwise stated. Fee schedules may be updated with 30 days' prior notice.

5 Revenue & Payouts

Revenue from ticket sales is held in escrow by our payment processor (Stripe Connect). Payouts are processed as follows:

  • Payouts are initiated within 5-7 business days after the event date.
  • For recurring events, payouts may be batched weekly.
  • Minimum payout threshold is $10.00 USD (or equivalent local currency).
  • Payouts are made to the bank account or payment method registered in your Merchant profile.
  • Event Waw reserves the right to withhold payouts in cases of suspected fraud, chargebacks, or policy violations.

6 Event Content & Compliance

Merchants are solely responsible for the content of their event listings. The following content is prohibited:

  • Events promoting illegal activities, violence, or discrimination.
  • Misleading or false event descriptions, images, or pricing.
  • Content that infringes on third-party intellectual property rights.
  • Events targeting or exploiting minors.
  • Spam, duplicate, or test events on the live platform.

Event Waw reserves the right to remove any event listing that violates these guidelines without prior notice.

7 Ticket Management

Merchants using the Platform's ticketing system agree to:

  • Set accurate ticket quantities that reflect actual venue capacity.
  • Not oversell beyond venue or safety limits.
  • Use the Platform's official QR code system for entry verification.
  • Not create or distribute duplicate or counterfeit tickets.
  • Clearly communicate ticket tiers, pricing, and any restrictions (e.g., age limits, dress codes).

8 Refunds & Cancellations

Merchants must establish a clear refund policy for their events. The following rules apply:

  • Merchant-cancelled events: Full refunds must be issued to all ticket holders within 14 days.
  • Postponed events: Attendees may request refunds if the new date is not suitable.
  • No-show events: If a Merchant fails to deliver the event, Event Waw will process automatic refunds and may suspend the Merchant account.
  • Partial cancellations: If specific tiers or features are cancelled, proportional refunds must be offered.

Event Waw may process refunds on behalf of non-responsive Merchants and deduct the amount from pending payouts.

9 Intellectual Property

By listing events on Event Waw:

  • You grant Event Waw a non-exclusive, worldwide license to display your event content (name, description, images) on the Platform and in marketing materials.
  • You represent that you own or have the right to use all content you upload.
  • Event Waw retains all rights to the Platform's design, code, branding, and proprietary technology.
  • You may not copy, modify, or reverse-engineer any part of the Platform.

10 Data Protection

Merchants handling attendee data must:

  • Comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).
  • Use attendee data solely for event-related purposes.
  • Not sell, share, or misuse attendee personal information.
  • Implement reasonable security measures to protect stored data.
  • Promptly notify Event Waw of any data breach involving attendee information.

For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.

11 Liability & Indemnification

The Merchant agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Event Waw, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from any claims, damages, losses, liabilities, or expenses arising from:

  • The Merchant's events, products, or services.
  • Any breach of this Agreement by the Merchant.
  • Any injury, damage, or loss occurring at the Merchant's events.
  • Any third-party claims related to the Merchant's content or activities.

Event Waw's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Merchant in the preceding 12 months.

12 Termination

This Agreement may be terminated:

  • By the Merchant: At any time by deleting your account, subject to fulfilling all outstanding event obligations.
  • By Event Waw: Immediately upon breach of this Agreement, or with 30 days' notice for any reason.

Upon termination, all pending payouts for completed events will be processed within 30 days. Any outstanding obligations, including refund responsibilities, survive termination.

13 Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved as follows:

  • Informal Resolution: Parties will first attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation within 30 days.
  • Mediation: If informal resolution fails, disputes will be submitted to mediation.
  • Governing Law: This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Arab Republic of Egypt.
